ITChatter: Mobile Device Security

Tuesday, November 15, 2011, 12:00 pm EST

Penn State's Ag IT team hosts lunch conversations about different technologies and their uses, called the ITChatter series. This month's session will be looking at Mobile Device Security. Are our smartphones too 'smart'? Vince Verbeke, Support Specialist with the college will share what he knows about protecting you and your device. Smartphones store your personal data and sensitive information. In this ITChatter session we will explore what vulnerabilities and risks are associated with these devices. We'll also look at twelve ways to help protect your smartphone. Participants are asked to arrive in the Adobe Connect meeting room (http://meeting.psu.edu/ITChatter) at noon. The chatter gets started at 12:15 and will wrap up by 12:50. You can join us each month or participate in just the ITChatters that interest you. Participants can login as a guest.

Getting the most out of Google Calendar

Friday, November 18, 2011, 2:00 pm EST

Learn how to use Google Calendar as your life's calendar. Create multiple calendars to organize your time and resources. Share calendars with the people you want to share with. If you have a personal Google account, learn how to separate your work calendar from your personal calendar. Learn how to use the Tasks feature to help you remember to do the things you need to get done.
